# Voice and claim policy

## Use

- built and maintain
- work on
- experimenting with
- public beta
- pre-launch
- fixture-verified
- published benchmark reports
- current limitation
- independent reproduction pending

## Avoid

- founder as the headline identity
- visionary
- revolutionary
- cutting-edge
- world-class
- production-grade without a published support definition
- secure without a stated threat model
- AI expert
- 10x or 100x engineer
- changing the world
- used by thousands unless measured and linked
- selected for or working at Gumloop; say participating in the learning cohort

## Claim template

For any important public claim, record:

1. Exact wording
2. Stable evidence URL
3. Revision or release
4. Environment
5. Known limitation
6. Whether it has been independently reproduced

## Editing test

Before adding a sentence, ask:

- Is this a claim or a description?
- What could a skeptical reader inspect?
- Is the lifecycle state obvious?
- Could this be read more broadly than the evidence supports?
- Would I say this sentence naturally in an interview?
